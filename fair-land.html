Fair Land - Coastal Residential Development

Project Gallery & Walkthrough

Fair Land is a prestigious residential project located on the ECR coast in Chennai. Our team handled the complete lifecycle of this project, from architectural design and structural engineering to interior finishing.

The goal was to create a modern, sustainable community that maximized natural light and coastal views. This involved using locally sourced, eco-friendly materials and incorporating rainwater harvesting systems.

Project Amenities

1. 24/7 Security and Gated Community

2. Blacktop Roads and Street Lights

3. Underground Drainage and Sewage System

4. Lush Green Parks and Children's Play Area

5. Access to Potable Water (Borewell)

6. Dedicated Transformer and Electricity Access

7. High-Speed Internet and Communication Lines

8. Rainwater Harvesting System

9. Clear Legal Documents and Title Deeds

10. Community Gathering Space
